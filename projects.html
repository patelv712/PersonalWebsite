Project 3: Rainzer

Purpose

Rainzer was one of the first projects I engaged in where I truly discovered my drive for working on items that will leave a positive impact on the world. With this hardware-oritened project, I was truly able to develop my passion for creating technologies that present solutions.

About

  • Chosen by Science Department to beta test research program
  • Worked with science department to identify real-world problems and to find a mentor to assist with solution
  • Developed solution to harvest rainwater and transform the kinetic energy into stored potential energy in a battery
  • Used CAD, Adobe Illustrato, and circuits to implement a solution to prove my concept

Future Features

  • Work on a larger scale to build a real-life version
    • My render model was able to power 3 small LEDSs, in the future I could try to light up a commercial bulb and eventually move onto a house.
